About ODES

ODES (Object Detection for Estrous Staging) is a machine learning tool designed to detect and classify cell types for monitoring the estrous cycle in female mice. This advanced tool assists in biological research and veterinary diagnostics by providing precise and automated analysis of cell types, which is crucial for studying reproductive health and various related phenomena.
